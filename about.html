About

Orie Maharaj Family Foundation(OMFF) is a family run 501(c)3 non-profit organization. We believe in Equality, Ambition to succeed, Responsibility to aid our fellow men, Togetherness against all odds and Humbleness towards all our neighbors, it stands for EARTH and our goal is ensure the health of mankind and our environment, our home. It not an easy or simple task but together we can achieve peace on Earth. It is our duty, our responsibility as it’s inhabitants and our unflinching dedication to that task will lead us to success, to eliminate poverty, ensure the health of our people and our planet. In so doing we can tackle socioeconomic, cultural, humanitarian and public health issues. Including: relief of the poor and distressed in emergency situations or the underprivileged by aiding families both foreign and domestic in uplifting there communities so they can have the ability to address attaining simple necessities, such as: food, clothing, housing, schools, water and much needed medicinal attention. Where there is will, there is a way and it can be done and will be done. Please join us in our mission, we would love to work with you.

Mission Statement

The core purpose of the Orie Maharaj Family Foundation is to solve the problems at the root of Global Public Health issues. The core strategies is to empower communities to recognize their own problems and strategically develop community management techniques to solve them. We believe “It is better to teach a man to fish than to give a man a fish”. We do believe in micromanagement but macromanagement, it is better to fix the underly community issues and uplift the community to decrease public health issues from an economic standpoint. Our primary role is to act as a guidance for communities and not to simply run project but implement standing health policies and health practices. It is our goal that whether our organization exist or not, the foundation we set will remain through echelons of the communities and population we impact.OMFF insists that a country’s healthcare foundation and development are universal development imperatives that are integral to human survival and is the key to addressing public health issues.OMFF mobilizes human will and material resources to help countries, particularly developing countries, ensure a health equity and to build their capacity to form appropriate policies and deliver services for county and communities. OMFF is committed to providing disaster assistance to decrease public health issues including pandemics and epidemics. OMFF responds in emergencies to help those in need. OMFF makes its unique and highly expertise staff in the field of public health available for rapid response. OMFF is non-partisan and its cooperation is free of discrimination. In everything it does, the most disadvantaged communities and the countries in greatest need have priority. OMFF aims, through its programmes, to promote the health equity and decrease health disparities and to support community efforts and full participation in the, social, economical, behavioral and developmental upliftment of their own communities. OMFF works with all its partners towards the attainment of the sustainable human development goals adopted by the world community and the realization of the vision of peace and social progress enshrined in the values of the Orie Maharaj Family Foundation.OMFF believes in Equality, Ambition to succeed, Responsibility to aid our fellow men, Togetherness against all odds and Humbleness towards all our neighbors, it stands for EARTH and our goal is ensure the health of mankind and our environment, our home.
