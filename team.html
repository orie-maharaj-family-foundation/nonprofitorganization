About What Makes Up Our Public Health Team

The Orie Maharaj Family Foundation(OMFF)team composes of professionals at all levels from a variety of backgrounds because to achieve our mission to solve public health problems at the root, we need everyone to help and everyone to work together to achieve a common goal of HEALTHCARE FOR ALL! That includes: community members,leaders,fathers, mothers, youth,teachers, physicians,anthroplogist,epidemiologist,data analyst, researchers,spatial data analyst,nutritionist, hygienist,sanitation officers, construction workers, mental health professional even religious workers all have a place in public health. We are ONE!
